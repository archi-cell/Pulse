What is Pulse

Your mind, measured
in 45 seconds.

Pulse uses four quick physical challenges to read your mental state — no questionnaires, no journaling, just interaction. It's the fastest honest check-in you'll find.

React

Tap when the orb lights. Your average response time measures nervous-system arousal.

Rhythm

Tap in sync with a pulse. Timing accuracy reveals internal tempo and steadiness.

Remember

Replay a colour sequence. Working-memory accuracy signals deliberate focus.

Hold Still

Press and hold for two seconds. Physical restlessness reflects anxiety level.

How scoring works

01

Four challenges, four signals

Each challenge contributes a score from 0–100 across reaction, steadiness, memory, and stillness axes.

02

Combined into a mood reading

Total scores map to five states — Calm, Focused, Balanced, Wired, or Restless. You can override the result.

03

Morning and evening slots

Two check-ins per day build a richer picture of how your state shifts across the day.

04

26 weeks of history

A colour-coded grid shows every entry. Tap any day for detail. Export to CSV anytime.
